Our Commitment

DrySolve Restoration is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. We aim to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards.

Features

  • Semantic HTML and proper heading structure
  • Sufficient color contrast in our design system
  • Keyboard-navigable forms and interactive elements
  • Descriptive link text and form labels
  • Responsive design that scales to user-set font sizes
  • Alt text on informational images

Known Limitations

Despite our efforts, some content may not yet be fully accessible. We are actively working to improve. If you encounter accessibility barriers on our site, please let us know — we want to fix them.
