Why DrySolve Exists

Most homeowners and property managers first encounter restoration during the worst day of their year. A burst pipe at midnight. A hailstorm that takes out a roof. A sprinkler discharge that floods three floors of a hotel.

In those moments, the difference between a national call-center brand and a local team who knows the area is enormous. National brands often dispatch through unfamiliar subcontractors. Local mom-and-pop shops can't always staff 24/7. DrySolve was built to be both: locally owned and operated, with the resources, certifications, and SLAs of a national network.

Our Standards

  • 60-minute response commitment on emergency calls
  • Owned equipment — we don't wait on rentals when minutes matter
  • Xactimate-compatible documentation for seamless insurance claims
  • Direct insurance billing with major carriers
  • Transparent communication from first call to final walkthrough

Built to Scale

DrySolve is structured as a franchise network so that every market we enter is run by a local owner-operator who knows their community. Behind them is a national platform: technology, training, marketing, and vendor relationships that no individual restoration company could build alone.

It's the model that turns a local trade into a real business — and turns a worst-day-of-the-year customer experience into something a homeowner will recommend to a neighbor.
