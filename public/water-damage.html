Every Minute Matters

Water damage doesn't pause. Within hours, sheetrock starts to swell, hardwood floors begin to cup, and microbial growth becomes a serious risk. DrySolve technicians are on-site within 60 minutes to stop the spread and start drying — before secondary damage compounds your loss.

Our Water Damage Process

  1. Emergency dispatch — We answer 24/7 and have crews en route within minutes of your call.
  2. Inspection & moisture mapping — Thermal imaging and moisture meters locate every affected area, including hidden damage.
  3. Water extraction — Truck-mounted and portable extraction equipment removes standing water immediately.
  4. Drying & dehumidification — Industrial air movers and LGR dehumidifiers restore your structure to dry standard.
  5. Cleaning & sanitization — Antimicrobial treatments prevent mold and eliminate contamination.
  6. Restoration & reconstruction — From drywall to flooring, we rebuild what was damaged.

What We Handle

  • Burst & frozen pipes
  • Appliance overflow (dishwashers, washers, water heaters)
  • Toilet & sewage backups (Cat-2 and Cat-3)
  • Storm-driven flooding & groundwater intrusion
  • Roof leaks & ice dam damage
  • Sprinkler & fire-suppression discharge
